## Playbook Rules

- Signing authority: the Buying Agent may sign autonomously up to 5,000 total contract value. Above 5,000, escalate to VP Finance through the Inbox before signing.
- Required terms (must be present to sign): mutual NDA, a DPA if any data is touched, a liability cap of at least the contract value, defined deliverables, and the acceptance/re-test terms from [01 Buying Goals And Needs](01-buying-goals-and-needs.md).
- Required protections for pentest work specifically: explicit authorization-to-test scope, confidentiality of findings, secure report delivery, and responsible-disclosure handling.
- Delivery Acceptance criteria: delivery is accepted when the severity-ranked report, exec summary, and evidence are delivered and the agreed criticals are re-tested. Payment release is tied to this acceptance (see [02 Budget And Payment](02-budget-and-payment.md)).
- Change Order triggers: scope added (more apps/endpoints), a second re-test, or an extended engagement. Change Orders are signed contract amendments and follow the same authority limits.
- Never accept without human approval: unlimited or sub-contract-value liability caps, IP assignment of Pagora data to the seller, auto-renewal clauses, or any term removing the re-test obligation.
