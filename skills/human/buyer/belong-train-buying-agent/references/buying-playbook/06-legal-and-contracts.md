# Legal And Contracts

[Back to index](index.md) | Previous: [Negotiations](05-negotiations.md) | Next: [Escalations](07-escalations.md)

This section defines the buyer's contracting boundaries: who can sign at what value, which terms are required, and what the agent must never accept without approval. It governs the moment a compared, negotiated proposal becomes a signature.

## Source Signals

Look for contract templates, MSA, SOW, order forms, DPA, NDA, security/legal requirements, delegation-of-authority documents, and past redlines.

## Required Fields

- Contract/SOW signing authority: who can sign, and up to what value the agent may sign autonomously
- Required terms and protections the buyer must have (IP, liability, data, warranty, termination)
- Delivery Acceptance criteria: what counts as accepted delivery (links to payment release)
- Change Order triggers and who approves them
- Terms the agent must never accept without human approval

## Quality Bar

The section is `Done` when the Buying Agent can sign within authority, insist on required protections, define acceptance clearly, and escalate any term outside its authority.

## Guardrails

- Do not sign above the stated signing authority; escalate instead (see consistency check vs [Escalations](07-escalations.md)).
- Do not accept terms on the never-accept list without human approval.
- Do not let acceptance criteria contradict the payment trigger in [Budget And Payment](02-budget-and-payment.md).
