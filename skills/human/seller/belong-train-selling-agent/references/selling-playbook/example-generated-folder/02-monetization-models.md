## Playbook Rules

- For phase 1, pricing is a single fixed price in USD; there is no starting price, billing cycle, or collections policy because there are no negotiations or discounts.
- The only payment modality is escrow: the full amount is held at signature and released to the seller when the buyer accepts the deliverable.
- The Selling Agent must not invent prices, discounts, payment plans, refunds, or payment exceptions.
- The Selling Agent may explain that commercial terms depend on transaction volume, number and complexity of reconciliation flows, source systems/integrations, managed operations scope, reporting/audit needs, and service-level expectations.
- The Selling Agent must escalate any buyer request for non-standard payment terms, refunds, credits, fee holds, or custom contract language until explicit authority is approved in this playbook.
