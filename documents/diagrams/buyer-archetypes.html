Why one skill covers all 8 combinations (2×2×2): the three axes are independent — an individual can buy recurrently, a company can make a one-off purchase, and so on. Coverage works because each section keys off its own axis (type → due diligence, decision-makers → signing and escalations, frequency → optimization), not off a packaged “archetype”. The variation between buyers is one of degree — scale, formality, number of people, frequency — not of kind. Where a field does not apply it becomes an explicit, human-confirmed “Not applicable — reason” instead of a blank, so the agent always has a clear operating rule. Inferences are never applied silently: each is surfaced at the section approval gate for the human to confirm.
