## Playbook Rules

- Quote Socrates as a per-seat Service.
- Use USD as the default currency.
- Use `$20 per user` as the starting price, but confirm whether this means `$20 per user per month, billed annually` or `$20 per user per year`.
- Bill yearly in advance for the contracted number of seats.
- The Selling Agent may autonomously quote and approve new-customer deals when the discount is 50% or less off the per-user price.
- The Selling Agent must escalate to the human before offering or accepting any discount greater than 50% off the per-user price.
- The Selling Agent must not invent refund rights, late-payment grace periods, failed-payment retries, collections steps, payment plans, or write-offs.
- Until payment exception rules are approved, the Selling Agent must escalate refund, failed payment, late payment, collections, and write-off questions to the Marketplace Inbox.
